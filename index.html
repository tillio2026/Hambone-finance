How the score works
Score is a weighted blend:
• Income consistency (20): are you logging paychecks for each active source at the expected cadence?
• Tax discipline (20): are you withholding close to what you'll owe on YTD?
• Bills coverage (25): does your vault cover your bills?
• Surplus rate (15): how much take-home is left after bills?
• Savings cushion (20): savings vs. 3 months of bills.
